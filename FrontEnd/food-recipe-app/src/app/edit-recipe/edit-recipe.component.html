Instructions on adding steps: Please write steps in the following format -

1. Take 200ml of water in an utensil.
2. Boil it in medium flame.
3. Put in some noodles into the boiling water, with some stirring using a spoon. This breaks the noodles in smaller parts, easy to consume.
4. After about 3-4 minutes of boiling, pour the Maggi Masala into the boiling water.
5. Stir to thoroughly mix the Masala with the noodles and cover with a lid for about 30 seconds.
6. Switch off the supply and pour the noodles into a bowl to enjoy the hot Maggi.
